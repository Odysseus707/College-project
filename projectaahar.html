OUR PROJECT

The lives of numerous people have been adversely affected by poverty, disrupting their livelihoods, whether they are migrant workers or peddlers. The difficult turn that life has taken has left them in a position where it has become too hard to arrange for their basic needs. Many people have died every single day due to starvation. If we’ve enjoyed our meals in our homes, savoring new dishes each day, there cannot be a better way to be grateful to the almighty than lending out a helping hand to the needy fellow who’s being tortured by an empty belly. We believe that we rise by lifting others and have come up with our novel endeavor to feed the affected families by providing them home-cooked meals for free purchased from suppliers at an affordable cost of Rs. 50/- per person.
